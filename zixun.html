平安讯科亮相金融科技与支付创新年会 荣膺金融科技跨界标杆奖

深圳平安讯科技术有限公司，前身为中国平安集团内部孵化的互联网项目， 2017年3月1日获得光启集团5.5亿元融资，并正式独立为平安集团控股公司。

平安讯科依托平安集团金融背景、光启集团超材料及超级WiFi技术，提出通讯+金融的运营方向，以创新为驱动力，通过共享网络、智能连接、智慧服务、创新金融，整合利用网络与数据资源，为用户、商家、政府等提供全面的智能网络服务解决方案。

平安讯科以成为“中国第一智能连接运营商”为愿景，不断完善产品战略，目前旗下产品包括：平安WiFi、平安优联、平安悦行-车载WiFi、平安随行MiFi等，细分场景、精细化运营实现战略布局。同时，平安讯科发起并成立了智能WiFi网络应用产业联盟，投入1亿元用于推动行业软硬件等方面的研发，整合WiFi生态链，推动新技术、新材料、新产品的升级，进而推动行业升级。

平安讯科积极履行企业社会责任，先后参与“瓷娃娃”和“留守儿童”等公益关怀项目，通过连接的力量，倡导更多人关注相关公益群体。

Shenzhen Ping An Xunke Technology Co., Ltd is officially established on March 1st, 2017 and is now a holding company of China Ping An Group. Our internet project was incubated by the parent company, China Ping An Group and successfully received 550 million financing from Kuang-Chi Group.

We have strong financingbackground from China Ping An Group and gain metamaterial resources and Super-WiFitechnology from Kuang-Chi Group. We believe in our proposed network+financeoperation business model. Through the establishment of sharing networkintelligent connection and service,financing innovation, and the integration of network and data resources, we aimto offer comprehensive network service solutions for individual users,businesses in different industry, and the government.

Our vision is to become thefirst intelligent internet service provider in China. We constantly improve ourproducts including Ping An WiFi, U-Link, Vehicle WiFi, MiFi and more. Differentproducts are adjusted for different target scenes and strategic layout.Meanwhile, we established and developed the IWA²---Intelligent WiFi ApplicationAlliance. With one billion of investment, we focus on hardware and softwareR&D for the industry. The goals include integrating WiFi ecological chain,improving new technology and material, upgrading new products, and driving theindustry forward.

Ping An Xunke embracesocial responsibility. We fully support philanthropic organizations and participatein projects including “China Dolls “and “Left Behind Children”. We hope thatour power of connection will raise more social attention and create a betterworld.
